## Typography
Heading Font: DM Sans Bold

Label Font: DM Sans Medium
Heading Style: Clean, tight letter-spacing, sentence case preferred. Large display headings for hero sections.

## Vibe
Quiet luxury meets natural craftsmanship. Austrian precision with the warmth of wood grain. The brand sits between a premium cycling manufacturer and an artisanal design studio — think Aesop meets Canyon Bikes. Generous white space, slow visual pacing, and a deep respect for materials. Nothing shouts; the product speaks through texture, form, and restraint. Tagline energy: "Ride Your Nature."

## Image Style
Warm, natural-light photography. Two modes: (1) clean studio side-views on neutral off-white backgrounds for product shots, and (2) outdoor lifestyle imagery in lush Austrian/alpine landscapes — green meadows, mountain passes, gravel roads. Close-up detail shots emphasize wood grain, carbon weave, and precision joinery. Color grading is warm and slightly desaturated. No harsh shadows, no neon. Matte finishes dominate. People are shown riding, not posing — candid, in motion, helmeted.

## Layout Principles
- Generous padding and white space everywhere
- Full-bleed hero imagery with centered text overlay
- Grid-based product cards with minimal decoration
- Sticky bottom bar on product pages (product name + CTA)
- Feature highlights in 3-column cards with square images
- Testimonials in large serif-like quote styling
- No borders on cards — color blocking with Surface tones instead
- Icons are thin-line, minimal, monochrome

## Brand Details
- Origin: Austria (made in Austria emphasis)
- Material story: Wood-composite frames, carbon forks/rims
- Trust signals: 5-year warranty, 30-day returns, free shipping AT/DE
- Awards: German Design Award, Eurobike Award, ISPO Brand New
- Tone of voice: Confident but unhurried. Poetic without being pretentious. Short declarative sentences. "What worked 500 years ago still works today."
